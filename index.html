Our pizza is absolutely world class! With only the highest-quality, freshest ingredients available, and made to order. We have an assortment of traditional and unique toppings to choose from, so you can build your pizza just the way you like!

Crust

Choose your crust just the way you like! We have a variety of different styles to choose from that include hand-tossed, thin crust, pan-style, brick-oven, deep-dish, and of course our famous Italiano style crust.

Cheeses

We have over a dozen different cheeses to choose from here at Mikey's Pizzeria. We have the traditional mozzarella, provolone, and chedder, but we also have unique blends such as asiago, fontina, and gruyere.

Veggies

We serve only the freshest vegetables on our pizza here at Mikey's Pizzeria. We have all the expected toppings a traditional pizzeria would have, but we also have toppings such as grilled zucchini, kalamata olives, and caramalized onions.

Meats

We believe the more meat the better! That's why we offer over a dozen different meats to choose from! We have plethora of unique toppings such as sweet southern BBQ, carribean jerk chicken, and smoked brisket style pizza.
